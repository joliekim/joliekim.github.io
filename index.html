I am currently an HCI researcher and a MSc. candidate in the Department of Industrial Design at KAIST, where I explore the intersection of physical and digital interaction in Make Lab led by Prof. Andrea Bianchi.


My research rethinks how people interact with intelligent tools—especially in the creative sector—by replacing monolithic human-AI systems with "atomic" interaction patterns that are quick, controllable, composable and ready for real creative workflows.


Before joining KAIST, I earned my B.S. in Culture & Design Management from Yonsei University, working across AdTech (Moloco), Blockchain (Kakao Corp.), and F&B — co-founding NOT BASIC.
