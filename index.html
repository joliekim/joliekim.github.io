I am an HCI researcher and a MSc. candidate in the Department of Industrial Design at KAIST, where I explore the intersection of physical and digital interaction in Make Lab led by Prof. Andrea Bianchi.


My research aims to rethink how people interact with intelligent tools—especially in the creative sector—by replacing monolithic human-AI systems with atomic interaction patterns that are more controllable and composable. So I build tools and systems that empower users in their creative processes.


Before joining KAIST, I earned my B.S. in Culture & Design Management from Yonsei University, working across AdTech and Blockchain, also founding an F&B startup.
